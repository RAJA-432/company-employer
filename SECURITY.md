Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow the steps below:

Report Location: Submit the vulnerability report through the official security contact channel (e.g., security@project.org).

Acknowledgment: You will receive an acknowledgment within 48 hours confirming receipt of your report.

Investigation: The security team will investigate the issue and may request additional information.

Updates: Expect regular updates on the status of your report, typically every 7 days until resolution.

Resolution: If the vulnerability is accepted, a patch will be developed and released as soon as possible. If declined, you will be provided with a clear explanation.

Disclosure: Coordinated disclosure will be followed to ensure users are protected before public announcements.

Expectations

Accepted Vulnerabilities: A fix will be prioritized and released in the next supported version update.

Declined Vulnerabilities: Clear reasoning will be communicated to the reporter.

Communication: Transparency and timely communication are key principles of our security process.
